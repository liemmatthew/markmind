#MMForGood

MarkMind believes in paying it forward and giving back to the community.

Yes, companies exist for customers and investors. But they are not the only stakeholders in a business. A company is also for its employees (and their families), and for the communities they serve.

Our commitment for a better us

In Indonesia, organizations that strive for positive impact (such as nonprofits and NGOs) often lack the digital marketing skills needed to reach their target audience and raise awareness for their cause.


At MarkMind, we believe that this gap in skills should not exist. We are committed to empowering Indonesia's nonprofits and NGOs through #MMForGood. It isn't just a trend or a hashtag. It's our hope for a better future!

By using our services, you become a part of #MMForGood


How? A portion of our proceeds-included in our service's price-goes to the digital advancements of social enterprises in Indonesia.


Let's collaborate!
